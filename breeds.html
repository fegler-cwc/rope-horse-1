Lesson Sequence

1. Gaining Attention: "The Speed vs. Power" visual analysis hook.
2. Stimulate Recall: Brief review of equine anatomy (Withers, Scapula, Hindquarters).
3. Present Content: Interactive 15-Breed Gallery Walk.
4. Elicit Performance: "The Breed Matchmaker" activity and Math Lab.

Core Terminology

Hand: 4 inches. Unit of measure.

Withers: Highest point of vertebrae.

Conformation: Skeletal structure.

Gait: Rhythmic leg movement.

Feathering: Long hair on lower legs.

Sclera: White portion of the eye.

Suggested Activities

Activity 1: The Breed Matchmaker

Assign students a scenario (e.g., "A rancher in high altitude needs a horse for cattle"). Students must select 3 of the 15 breeds and justify their picks based on physical traits.

Activity 2: Flashcard Relay

Print the breed cards from the catalog. Have students race to categorize them into "Hot-blooded" vs "Cold-blooded" groups.
